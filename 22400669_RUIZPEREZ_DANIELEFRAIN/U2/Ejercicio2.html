Neffex

NEFFEX is an American music project, founded in 2014, led by Bryce Savage that originally included Cam Wales as well. Since the beginning, it was their goal to motivate and inspire people above all else. This resulted in the incredible achievement of releasing 100 Songs in 100 Weeks, all copyright-free (under a Creative Commons license), before collaborating with a label to release an EP followed by a full studio album. After the release of two singles and one collaboration in early 2021, Cam decided to leave the project to pursue his own music. Bryce continued to release music under the NEFFEX name, and after a collaboration with Frank Zummo, Bryce Savage returned to copyright-free music with releasing 100 Songs in 100 Weeks all over again, starting July 14, 2021. Starting August 23, 2021, Bryce started to release songs under his own name. These songs are meant to be more personal and meant to make the listener feel something, rather than motivational or inspirational themes. Even after finishing releasing 100 Songs in 100 Weeks all over again, Bryce continued to regularly release copyright-free music under the NEFFEX name, with no intention of stopping soon. NEFFEX, to this day, continues to inspire and motivate millions of people and artists worldwide.
